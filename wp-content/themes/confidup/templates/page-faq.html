SEO

What is SEO and why does it matter?

SEO (Search Engine Optimisation) is the process of improving your website so it ranks higher in Google and other search engines for relevant queries. Higher rankings mean more organic (unpaid) traffic, more leads, and more revenue — without paying for every click.

How long does SEO take to show results?

SEO is a long-term investment. Most clients begin to see meaningful improvements within 3–6 months, with significant results typically achieved within 6–12 months. The timeline depends on your starting point, competition, and the scope of work.

What does your SEO service include?

Our SEO service covers technical audits and fixes, keyword research, on-page optimisation, content strategy, link building, Core Web Vitals improvements, and monthly performance reporting. The exact scope depends on your plan.

Do you guarantee first-page rankings?

No reputable SEO agency can guarantee specific rankings — search engines are constantly evolving and no one controls their algorithms. What we do guarantee is a data-driven strategy, transparent reporting, and a relentless focus on improving your results.

AEO

What is AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation)?

AEO is the practice of optimising your content to appear in answer-based search features like Google's featured snippets, People Also Ask boxes, and voice search results. It focuses on structuring content so search engines can extract and surface it as a direct answer.

How is AEO different from SEO?

SEO focuses on ranking pages in traditional search results. AEO goes further — it optimises for position zero (featured snippets), voice search, and question-based queries where search engines display a direct answer rather than a list of links. AEO and SEO work best together.

What results can I expect from AEO?

Our AEO clients typically see featured snippet wins within 3–4 months, alongside improvements in voice search visibility and People Also Ask coverage. One of our SaaS clients won 80+ featured snippets in 4 months, driving a 210% increase in trial signups.

GEO

What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation)?

GEO is the practice of optimising your brand's presence so it is cited and recommended by AI platforms like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Claude. As more people use AI to find answers and recommendations, GEO ensures your business is part of those answers.

How do you get a brand recommended by AI platforms?

AI platforms cite brands that are recognised as authoritative and trustworthy sources. We build this through entity optimisation, knowledge graph signals, high-authority content, digital PR, and structured data — positioning your brand as the definitive source in your niche.

How do you measure GEO success?

We monitor your brand's citation rate across major AI platforms, track brand mention growth, and measure downstream impact through referral traffic and lead attribution. We provide monthly GEO reports alongside your SEO and AEO reporting.
